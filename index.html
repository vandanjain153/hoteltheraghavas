Welcome

Your home of devotion & rest in Ayodhya

At Hotel The Raghavas', we believe a pilgrimage deserves a stay that is just as soulful. Set a short, peaceful walk from Shri Ram Janm Bhoomi and Hanuman Garhi, our hotel pairs the calm of the holy city with the comforts of a modern, well-kept retreat.

From dual-weather air conditioning and a treated fresh-air plant to thoughtful in-room dining, every detail is arranged so you can focus on what truly matters — your darshan and your peace of mind.
